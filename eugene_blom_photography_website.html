About

Eugene Blom

I'm an 18-year-old photographer based in Cape Town, South Africa — one of the most photographically rich places on earth. Growing up surrounded by the Fynbos, the Atlantic Ocean, and world-class nature reserves, I developed a deep obsession with capturing the natural world exactly as it is: unposed, unfiltered, and alive.

My work focuses primarily on wildlife and nature photography — from the caracals of the Cape Peninsula to the southern right whales that grace our shores each winter. I'm drawn to the quiet moments: a raptor mid-stoop, morning mist rolling over the Hottentots Holland mountains, a mongoose family going about their business in Kirstenbosch.

I also have a strong interest in documentary and editorial photography, particularly capturing the people, institutions, and operations that define our city and country. I believe powerful images tell stories that words simply can't.
